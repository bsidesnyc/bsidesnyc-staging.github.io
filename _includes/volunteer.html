Join our Slack and the general mailing list for all of the latest information and volunteer opportunities.

The majority of volunteer opportunities are for the day of the conference. A call for volunteers will be put out via Slack and the mailing list roughly one month prior to the conference date. Volunteers are given the choice of tasks such as managing speakers, distributing attendee materials, supporting sponsors, registering attendees, guiding attendees, and supporting various other activities!
